Books

There are more books than how many I'll list here, but the most known books are:

  • The Silmarillion
  • The Hobbit
  • The Fellowship of the Ring
  • The Two Towers
  • The Return of the King

These are the "main" books to Tolkien's world. These tell the most important tales of his world. Besides these, there are other books as well, but they are less known, and some would argue that they are not that important either.

Movies

The most known adaptations of Tolkien's books are the Lord of the Rings film trilogy:

  • The Fellowship of the Ring (2001)
  • The Two Towers (2002)
  • The Return of the King (2003)

And the Hobbit trilogy:

  • The Hobbit: An Unexpected Journey
  • The Hobbit: The Desolation of Smaug
  • The Hobbit: The Battle of the Five Armies
